Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2024

Welcome to Recycla Hub. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect and use the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other personal identifiers.
  • Activity Data: Records of your interactions with our services, including login history, consultations, and service requests.
  • Camera Data: Images and videos captured via the camera for specific services.
  • Location Data: Geographical location data if you have enabled location services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, respond to your requests, and communicate with you.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Financial transactions and billing information are retained for up to 7 years due to legal and regulatory requirements. Anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes.

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to https://recyclahub.co.za and log in with your account credentials.
  2. Navigate to Account Settings by clicking on your profile icon at the top right corner and selecting "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu.
  3. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section and click on the "Request Account Deletion" button.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm your request. You may be asked to re-enter your password or provide additional confirmation.

Once you have submitted your request, our team will process it, and you will receive a confirmation email. Your account and all associated data will be deleted permanently. If you change your mind, please contact our support team within 24 hours of your request.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team at info@recyclahub.co.za.

Types of Data Deleted

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and any other personal identifiers.
  • Activity Data: Records of your interactions with our services, including but not limited to login history, consultations, and service requests.
  • Saved Preferences: Any settings or preferences you have customized in your account.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@recyclahub.co.za.
